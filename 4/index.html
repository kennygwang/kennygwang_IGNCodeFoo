Bonus: Programmatically create a game of Connect 4 with one AI-controlled opponent and a human-controlled player.

This is a game of Connect 4 made using the HTML Canvas element and Javascript. Click one of the columns to drop in a piece. The human-controlled player is always the first to move. The AI-controlled opponent heuristically determines its next move based on where the human-controlled player's pieces are, and tends to be more defensive. It's not impossible to beat, but does requires the player to pay a fair amount of attention. The wins are recursively checked for.
