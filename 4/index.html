Bonus #1: Programmatically create a game of Connect 4 with one AI-controlled opponent and a human-controlled player.

This is a game of Connect 4 made using the HTML Canvas element and Javascript. Click on one of the columns to drop in a piece. The human-controlled player is always the first to move. The AI-controlled opponent's moves are heuristically determined. Its first priority is to win and its second priority is to block human-controlled player wins. Otherwise, it moves depending on how many human-controlled player pieces surround a particular spot. It avoids moves that give the player a chance to win and also avoids moves that give the player a chance to block. It's not impossible to beat, but it does require the player to pay attention. The wins are recursively determined. The scoreboard below the game board uses jQuery.
