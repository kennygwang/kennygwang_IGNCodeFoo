2. Create a liquid layout with HTML, CSS, and Javascript. This layout must support the following resolutions: 1024x768, 1680x1050. Explain why you would use a liquid layout.


A liquid layout makes your webpage adaptable and more useable. With a liquid layout, you do not have to re-design your website as the times change and different screen resolutions become more popular.
Useablility improves because a liquid layout cuts down the need for horizontal scroll bars, making it so that your content will display in its entirety for a wide range of browser window widths and screen resolutions.

My liquid layout supports 1024x768 and 1680x1050 resolutions because having viewed this webpage on screens of both resolutions (as well as 800x600) I can say that readability -- the most important quality of a layout -- is not compromised in any of these resolutions.
